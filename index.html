About Board Bazaar

Board Bazaar brings over 10 years of expertise in crafting exceptional custom interior and exterior signs that transform business spaces into powerful brand statements. Our specialized portfolio includes premium acrylic signage, dynamic LED displays, striking 3D metal letters, and comprehensive sign solutions tailored to your unique business needs.

We pride ourselves on using only the highest quality materials combined with our experienced team's craftsmanship to deliver sign products that stand the test of time. From concept to installation, every project reflects our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and understanding of how effective signage drives business growth and brand recognition.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What types of signboards do you create?

We create all types of signboards including LED signs, neon signs, 3D signs, digital displays, and traditional printed signboards for various industries.

How long does the typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but most standard signboard projects are completed within 7-14 days from design approval to installation.

Do you provide maintenance services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance and repair services to ensure your signboards remain in optimal condition throughout their lifespan.

What materials do you use?

We use high-quality materials including aluminum, acrylic, LED modules, and weather-resistant coatings to ensure durability and longevity.

Do you serve businesses outside your local area?

Yes, we provide services nationwide and have partnerships with local installation teams to serve clients across multiple locations.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer comprehensive warranty packages ranging from 1-5 years depending on the type of signboard and materials used.
